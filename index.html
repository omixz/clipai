Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes — your first video is free, no card needed. A quick Google sign-in may be required to keep the free tier fair. Pro ($15/mo) removes the limit and the watermark.

How long does it take?

A few minutes per video. Your video enters a queue and the page updates live — you can leave it open and come back.

What happens to my video?

It's processed automatically and deleted from our servers within 24 hours. Nothing is used for anything except making your clips.

What videos work best?

Talking content with clear speech: podcasts, interviews, talks, streams, sermons, lectures. Music-only or silent footage won't produce good clips.

Can I dub my clips into another language?

Yes — Pro plans can dub English-source clips into Spanish, French, or Portuguese: the speech is translated and re-voiced, and the captions are translated too.

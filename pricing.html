Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans or cancel anytime?

Yes. Paid plans renew monthly but you can cancel anytime from your billing portal. We won't charge you for the next month — your plan stays active through the end of the current billing cycle.

What if I don't use all my monthly videos?

That's fine. Your monthly allocation resets on the same date each month. Unused videos don't roll over, but you'll have a fresh batch next month.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through PayPal. All payments are secure and encrypted — we never store your card details.

Do you offer refunds?

Since Peakcut processes videos immediately, refunds aren't available. However, you can cancel your plan anytime and won't be charged again.

What if I need more than 50 videos/month?

Reach out to support@peakcut.example — we can discuss custom plans for high-volume users and teams.

What's the difference between Pro and Pro Plus?

Pro gives you 20 videos/month — perfect for most creators uploading a weekly podcast or series. Pro Plus gives you 50/month — ideal for teams managing multiple channels or daily uploads. Both include all the same features (dubbing, multi-format, watermark-free).
